## How to Use These Documents

**For a Business Plan:** Read documents 01-06 in order. They follow the standard structure: What is it → Who needs it → How big is the market → How do we make money → What does it cost → How do we grow.

**For a Financial Model:** Focus on documents 04 (Revenue Model) and 05 (Cost Structure). These contain the specific numbers, assumptions, and formulas needed to build projections in a spreadsheet.

**For an Investor Deck:** Pull the narrative from 01 (Vision), the urgency from 02 (Pain Points), the opportunity from 03 (Market), the business model from 04 (Revenue), and the strategy from 06 (Go-to-Market).

**For Product Strategy:** Focus on documents 02 (Value Proposition), 07 (Roadmap), and 08 (Technical Architecture).
